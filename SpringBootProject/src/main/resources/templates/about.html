Family owned for more than 10 years!


On March 17th, 2009, Frosty Treats was opened in Boston Massachusetts and has grown to be the number one Ice Cream shop within the city. The companies vision of bringing the community together has been brought to fruition as Boston has voted us the best Ice Cream parlor in the area for 3 straight years! We run our company based off ideals of satisfying our customer's before anything else! We always look to give back to the community we love by throwing free Ice Cream parties for local elementary schools, or helping out those in need. We hope to continue growing and bringing the community together by serving the best Ice Cream with the best customer service!
